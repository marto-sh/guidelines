# Architecture Decision Record (ADR) Template

## Title: [ADR Title]

*Status*: [Proposed/Accepted/Rejected/Deprecated/Superseded by NNNN]
*Date*: [YYYY-MM-DD]
*Author*: [Your Name]

## Context

Describe the forces, concerns, and background that led to the decision. What is the problem we're trying to solve? What are the relevant factors and assumptions?
*   What is the problem we're trying to solve?
*   What are the business and technical goals?
*   What are the existing constraints (technical, organizational, time, budget)?
*   What are the relevant stakeholders and their concerns?

## Decision

State the decision in a clear, concise manner. What is the chosen solution?

## Alternatives Considered

Describe the alternatives that were evaluated. For each alternative, briefly explain:
*   Its main features or approach.
*   The pros (advantages, benefits) compared to the chosen decision.
*   The cons (disadvantages, risks, trade-offs) compared to the chosen decision.

## Consequences

Describe the results of the decision. What are the implications for the system, team, and future development?
*   **Positive**: What are the benefits and desirable outcomes?
*   **Negative**: What are the drawbacks, trade-offs, and potential new problems?
*   **Neutral**: What remains unchanged?
*   **Architectural Implications**: How does this decision affect the system's architecture, other components, or future design choices?
*   **Development Implications**: How does this decision affect the development process, tools, or team structure?
*   **Operational Implications**: How does this decision affect deployment, monitoring, or maintenance?

## Future Considerations

Are there any open questions or potential future work related to this decision?
